# Geometry Basics

## Triangles

The **sum of interior angles** in any triangle is $180°$.

For a right triangle with legs $a$, $b$ and hypotenuse $c$, the **Pythagorean theorem** states:

$$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$

### Area formulas

| Shape | Formula |
|-------|---------|
| Triangle | $A = \frac{1}{2} b h$ |
| Rectangle | $A = l \times w$ |
| Circle | $A = \pi r^2$ |
| Trapezoid | $A = \frac{(a + b)}{2} h$ |

## Circles

For a circle of radius $r$:

- **Circumference**: $C = 2\pi r$
- **Area**: $A = \pi r^2$
- **Arc length** for central angle $\theta$ (in radians): $s = r\theta$

## The Coordinate Plane

The **distance** between points $(x_1, y_1)$ and $(x_2, y_2)$:

$$d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}$$

The **midpoint**:

$$M = \left(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2},\ \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right)$$

## Similar Triangles

Two triangles are **similar** if their corresponding angles are equal. Their corresponding sides are then proportional:

$$\frac{a_1}{a_2} = \frac{b_1}{b_2} = \frac{c_1}{c_2}$$
